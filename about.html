About Msafari Tours

Your trusted partner for authentic East African adventures

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by passionate conservationists and travel experts, Msafari Tours was born from a simple belief: that tourism can be a powerful force for good when done responsibly. "Msafari" means "traveler" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to creating meaningful connections between visitors and the incredible landscapes, wildlife, and cultures of East Africa.

Based in Kampala, Uganda, with offices in the UK, we combine deep local knowledge with international expertise to create transformative travel experiences. Every tour we design supports conservation efforts and community development, ensuring that your adventure contributes to the preservation of East Africa's natural and cultural heritage.
