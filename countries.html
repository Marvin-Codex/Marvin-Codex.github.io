Serengeti Safari Experience

Witness the greatest wildlife spectacle on Earth - the Great Migration. Over 2 million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles move across the endless plains in search of fresh grazing, followed by predators in nature's most dramatic cycle.

Migration Seasons

  • • Dec-Mar: Calving season
  • • Apr-Jun: Moving north
  • • Jul-Oct: River crossings
  • • Nov: Return south

Safari Highlights

  • • Mara River crossings
  • • Cultural experiences

Conquer Africa's highest peak and one of the Seven Summits. Our experienced guides will lead you through five distinct climate zones to the "Roof of Africa" at 19,341 feet. No technical climbing experience required.
