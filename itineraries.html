15-Day Uganda Wildlife Safari

Experience the ultimate East African adventure with our comprehensive 15-day wildlife safari. Journey through Uganda's most spectacular national parks, encounter mountain gorillas, track the Big Five, and witness the dramatic Murchison Falls. This carefully crafted itinerary combines luxury accommodations with authentic wildlife experiences.

  • • Kibale Forest National Park

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Arrival in Kampala

Welcome to Uganda! Upon arrival at Entebbe International Airport, you'll be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel in Kampala. Evening briefing about your upcoming adventure.

Accommodation: Kampala Serena Hotel | Meals: Dinner
2

Transfer to Murchison Falls National Park

Early morning departure to Murchison Falls National Park. Stop at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for rhino tracking on foot. Continue to the park and visit the top of the magnificent Murchison Falls.

Accommodation: Paraa Safari Lodge | Meals: All meals
3

Game Drive & Nile Boat Safari

Morning game drive in the northern sector, searching for elephants, giraffes, lions, and buffalos. Afternoon boat cruise on the Nile to the base of Murchison Falls, with excellent bird watching opportunities.

Accommodation: Paraa Safari Lodge | Meals: All meals
4

Transfer to Kibale Forest

Journey to Kibale Forest National Park, known as the primate capital of the world. Afternoon at leisure to explore the lodge surroundings.

Accommodation: Primate Lodge | Meals: All meals

The complete 15-day itinerary includes chimpanzee tracking, Queen Elizabeth National Park, gorilla trekking in Bwindi, and Lake Mburo National Park.
